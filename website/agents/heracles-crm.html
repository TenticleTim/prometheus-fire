Mythology

Heracles completed twelve labors — not twelve glorious battles, but twelve systematic, unglamorous tasks that required method over strength. Cleaning the Augean stables in a single day was not a feat of force; it required redirecting two rivers. Heracles solved problems through relentless, structured effort that others found beneath them.

Thesmos channels Heracles as CRM and sales pipeline management. CRM data hygiene is the Augean stable: the accumulation of duplicate contacts, stale opportunities, and missing field values that make forecasts meaningless. Heracles does not complain about the mess — he builds the system that clears it systematically and keeps it clean.

Purpose
What Heracles does

Heracles designs and operates the CRM infrastructure that makes revenue predictable. He audits data hygiene, defines pipeline stages with objective exit criteria, builds lead scoring models that separate qualified from noise, and configures forecast categories that leadership can actually trust. Where other agents build the product or close the deal, Heracles ensures the system of record reflects reality — and that revenue operations run on signal, not noise.

Responsibilities
Heracles's domain
  • CRM data hygiene audit: duplicate detection, field completion rates, stale opportunity identification, and enrichment gaps
  • Pipeline stage definitions with objective entry and exit criteria — eliminates deals sitting in "Proposal" for 90 days
  • Lead scoring model design: behavioral + firmographic scoring, MQL/SQL threshold calibration, and routing rules
  • Forecast category configuration: Commit / Best Case / Pipeline / Omit with confidence weighting per stage
  • Salesforce and HubSpot configuration audits: field mapping, workflow automation, lifecycle stage sync, and reporting accuracy
  • CRM data architecture for new product lines or market segments: object model, custom fields, and relationship mapping
  • Revenue operations reporting: pipeline coverage ratios, win rate by segment/source/rep, average cycle length, and leading indicators

Example deliverables
CRM data hygiene audit report
Duplicate contact count, field completion rates by record type, stale opportunity analysis (days since last activity by stage), and a prioritized cleanup backlog with the revenue impact of each fix.
Pipeline stage definitions with exit criteria
Named stages with objective entry requirements, exit criteria that any rep can verify independently, and the CRM field or activity that triggers each stage transition — eliminates the subjective pipeline that produces inaccurate forecasts.
Lead scoring model + MQL/SQL thresholds
Behavioral and firmographic scoring weights, threshold definitions for MQL and SQL with the specific data points that inform each, routing rules to the correct rep or queue, and a 90-day recalibration schedule.
