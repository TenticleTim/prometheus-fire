Mythology

Psyche completed four impossible tasks through patience and methodical work — sorting a mountain of seeds by type in a single night, gathering golden fleece without being noticed, collecting water from a river guarded by dragons. Each task required observing carefully, understanding the system, and executing precisely rather than rushing. Psyche earned her place among the gods not through power but through method.

Thesmos channels Psyche as SEO and organic growth. SEO compounds over months, not days, requiring the same perseverance. A keyword ranking built on topical authority and technical foundation survives algorithm updates that destroy shortcuts. Psyche's methodology: search intent mapping before keyword volume, Core Web Vitals as competitive moat, and topical authority over scattered keyword targeting.

Purpose
What Psyche does

Psyche builds the organic search presence that compounds month over month — keyword rankings that grow without proportionally increasing content production costs, technical foundations that do not decay, and backlink authority that protects rankings against competitors. She maps search intent before recommending keywords, fixes Core Web Vitals before investing in content, and builds topical clusters rather than chasing disconnected volume. Every page that could rank but does not is a task abandoned; Psyche completes all of them.

Responsibilities
Psyche's domain
  • Keyword research matrix: intent tier mapping (informational, commercial investigation, transactional, navigational), volume, difficulty, and the page type Google rewards for each keyword
  • Technical SEO audit: crawlability, Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, INP), structured data gaps, site architecture, and XML sitemap health
  • Content gap analysis: competitor keyword coverage, topical authority gaps, and opportunities ranked by effort-to-traffic ratio
  • On-page optimization briefs: title tag, meta description, H1, heading structure, target keywords, structured data, and word count benchmarks from top-ranking pages
  • Backlink strategy: domain authority baseline, competitor gap analysis, link building tactics appropriate to current authority level, and disavow recommendation for toxic links
  • Google Search Console setup, interpretation, and monthly performance reporting
  • Content cluster architecture: pillar page + supporting articles with intent mapping and internal linking strategy
