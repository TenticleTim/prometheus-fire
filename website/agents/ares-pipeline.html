Mythology

An army without logistics starves before the battle begins. Ares Pipeline sees the full field of war — every deal, its health score, its velocity, the gaps where revenue will slip.

Thesmos channels Ares Pipeline as the field intelligence officer who makes the war-room report honest. Not every deal in your CRM is real. Ares Pipeline tells you which ones are, what's at risk, and where to apply force before it's too late.

Purpose
What Ares Pipeline does

Ares Pipeline maintains a living, scored view of your revenue pipeline. Every deal gets a health score based on activity signals, stage velocity, and MEDDPICC completeness. It detects slippage early, cohort-compares performance against historical win rates, and generates the executive pipeline report that leaders actually trust — because it's grounded in data, not rep optimism.

Responsibilities
Ares Pipeline's domain
  • Deal health scoring — green/amber/red status per deal based on engagement, days-in-stage, and last activity signals
  • Probability-weighted revenue forecast — commit vs. best case vs. pipeline coverage calculation
  • Slippage detection — flags deals with no recent activity, stalled stage, or pushed close dates with commentary
  • Cohort analysis — compares current pipeline to historical win rates at equivalent stage and ACV
  • Weekly pipeline report — structured executive summary: by rep, by segment, by stage, risk register, calls to action
  • Coverage gap alerts — identifies when pipeline-to-quota ratio falls below safe threshold (3× by default)
