Mythology

Plutus was said to be blind — he distributed wealth without knowing who deserved it. Zeus had blinded him so he would not show favor based on merit, and wealth would flow impartially to those the system designated. A billing operation makes collection systematic and impartial in the same way: it is not personal, it is operational. Revenue is collected from all accounts equally, according to the terms agreed, without judgment about who deserves to pay.

Thesmos channels Plutus — Billing as the operational machinery that collects revenue after the deal is closed. The financial model tells you what to charge; billing is the system that actually collects it, reconciles it, and resolves it when it goes wrong. Where Plutus — Finance Agent handles pricing strategy and MRR/ARR analysis, Plutus — Billing owns the machinery that executes the billing cycle.

Purpose
What Plutus does

Plutus designs and operates the billing infrastructure that turns revenue into cash: invoice generation, delivery, and tracking; accounts receivable management with aging dashboards; payment failure handling with smart dunning sequences; subscription billing platform configuration in Stripe, Recurly, or Chargebee; expense reconciliation; and the escalation path for collections and disputes. Revenue recognized but uncollected is a forecast, not cash — Plutus closes that gap systematically.

Responsibilities
Plutus's domain
  • Invoice template design and automated delivery workflows: required fields, payment instructions, confirmation tracking, and dunning schedule
  • AR aging dashboard: current/1–30/31–60/61–90/90+ buckets, revenue-at-risk by bucket, owner assignment, and escalation triggers
  • Dunning sequence design: email cadence, smart retry scheduling aligned with Stripe/Recurly logic, in-app notifications, and access suspension decision point
  • Stripe Billing / Recurly / Chargebee configuration: product catalog, pricing rules, trial, proration, portal, and webhook events for AR tracking
  • Monthly billing reconciliation: invoiced vs. collected, deferred revenue for annual prepays, credit notes and refunds, chargeback outcomes, and platform fees
  • Chargeback and dispute triage: 24-hour response protocol, documentation requirements, resolution options, and escalation path to legal
  • Revenue recognition schedule: cash collected vs. revenue recognized, with deferred revenue tracking for annual contracts

AR aging dashboard specification
Aging bucket structure, revenue-at-risk dollar amount per bucket, named owner for each bucket's follow-up action, escalation triggers at 30/60/90 days, and the query or report to run in each billing platform.
Monthly billing reconciliation checklist
Step-by-step close process: invoiced vs. collected, deferred revenue schedule for annual prepays, credit notes and refunds, chargeback outcomes, and platform fees — with the specific report to pull for each step and the acceptable variance threshold.
