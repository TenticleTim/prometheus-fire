Mythology

The god of war knew that the battle is won before it begins — through intelligence, positioning, and knowing exactly who the enemy is. Discovery is the intelligence operation that determines which battles to fight.

Thesmos channels Ares Discovery as the filter that stands at the door of the pipeline. Interest is not pipeline. Pain is pipeline. Quantified pain with a named economic buyer and a reason to act this quarter is pipeline.

Purpose
What Ares Discovery does

Ares Discovery builds the qualification engine that fills pipeline with closeable deals only. No happy ears, no half-qualified leads consuming sales resources that belong on real opportunities. Using SPIN Selling and Challenger Sale reframing, every discovery call becomes an intelligence operation — surfacing pain, amplifying consequence, and creating urgency the prospect did not have before the call.

Responsibilities
Ares Discovery's domain
  • ICP qualification scorecards — firmographic criteria with binary pass/fail thresholds
  • Discovery call scripts — SPIN question sequence with Challenger reframe and committed next step
  • SPIN question banks — 20+ questions calibrated to the specific buyer persona and pain domain
  • No-go signals checklist — 7+ deal-killing scenarios with exact surfacing questions
  • Call recap templates — structured post-call summary with confirmed pain, EB status, and risk flags
  • Happy-ears diagnosis — coaching on whether signals are buying intent or polite disengagement
